The TVL Strand



The Technical and Vocational Livelihood (TVL) strand is one of the LCBA's Senior High School (SHS) offerings. It focuses on helping students acquire practical, technical, and vocational skills so they can find work or launch their own enterprises after senior high school.

Giving students the information and abilities required for particular crafts and industries is its goal

Specializations include Home Economics (e.g., Cookery, Food Processing) and ICT (e.g., Computer Programming, Web Design).


Why choose the TVL Strand?


Job readiness:

TVL graduates are more employable since they have experience and practical skills.


Direct Path to

Particularly for trades and technical positions, TVL education may be the most direct route to work in several fields.


Enterpreneurship

The information and abilities required to launch a small business or work for yourself can also be obtained through TVL.
